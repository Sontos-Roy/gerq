What is abnormal esophageal acidity?

It is defined when the acid level in the esophagus increases beyond normal levels for extended periods. It is considered pathological when these levels remain abnormal for a significant time.

What is reflux esophagitis?

Reflux esophagitis is the most common injury that can occur as a consequence of GERD. It can be classified into different degrees of severity.
